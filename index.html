PROJECTS

Check out some of the case studies I made as a Data Analyst.

  • Banking Analysis

    I created an interactive dashboard that lets banks evaluate client profiles to make smart loan approval decisions and minimize lending risks. It allows users to track total loans, deposits, and fee structures dynamically, while segmenting client behaviors by income bands and demographics.

  • Basket analysis

    I created an interactive Power BI dashboard that lets retailers uncover hidden purchase patterns and find out which products customers frequently buy together. It allows users to track critical affinity metrics like support, confidence, and lift to build smart product bundles and optimize store layouts.

  • BlinkIT Analysis

    I built a Python script that cleans raw Blinkit sales data and exports the final metrics for easy integration. It lets users track grocery sales, outlet performance, and consumer trends by transforming messy spreadsheets into structured, production-ready data.

  • Cricket Analysis Dashboard

    I created an interactive Power BI dashboard that lets sports analysts and team managers evaluate cricket player performances to select the ultimate playing 11. It allows users to filter and track key player statistics—like strike rates, batting averages, and bowling economies—to easily identify match-winners and consistent performers.

  • Customer Shopping Behaviour

    I created an interactive Power BI dashboard that lets retail businesses dive deep into customer shopping behavior to understand exactly what drives sales. It allows users to track spending patterns and segment buyers by demographics, product categories, and promotional responses to build highly targeted marketing campaigns.

  • Health care Analysis

    I created an interactive analytics dashboard for Papollo Hospital that lets healthcare administrators track patient flow, monitor bed occupancy, and evaluate department performance. It allows users to analyze critical hospital KPIs—like emergency room wait times, average length of stay, and patient satisfaction scores—to optimize staffing and improve care delivery.

  • Hospitality Analysis

    I created an interactive dashboard that lets revenue managers in the hospitality industry reclaim market share and optimize pricing strategies.

  • Regional Sales Analysis

    I created an interactive sales dashboard that lets retail companies track geographic revenue trends and evaluate regional manager performance. It allows users to dive deep into local sales data, segmenting top-performing product categories, profit margins, and distribution channels across different territories to identify growth opportunities and optimize supply chains.

  • Walmart Data Analytics

    This project let users look at how different store departments are performing, track weekly sales growth, and see how holidays affect shopping habits across different store locations.
